# MBBS SEO Roadmap

## Goal

Make Students Traffic the strongest MBBS-abroad decision resource for Indian students first, then expand into adjacent study-abroad verticals later.

For now the domain should behave like an MBBS-first brand, not a broad study-abroad portal.

## Core strategy

Build two connected search graphs:

1. National MBBS authority pages
2. Tamil Nadu local-intent pages

The local graph should support the national graph, not compete with it.

## National MBBS clusters

### 1. Decision pages

- MBBS Abroad for Indian Students
- Is MBBS Abroad Worth It?
- MBBS Abroad vs Private MBBS in India
- Best Countries for MBBS Abroad

### 2. Cost pages

- MBBS Abroad Fees 2026
- MBBS Abroad Under 25 Lakhs
- MBBS Abroad Under 35 Lakhs
- Hidden Costs of MBBS Abroad
- Hostel and Living Cost Guide

### 3. Compliance pages

- NMC Rules for MBBS Abroad
- WDOMS vs NMC Recognition
- NExT After MBBS Abroad
- Return to India After MBBS Abroad
- Internship After MBBS Abroad

### 4. Country pages

- MBBS in Russia
- MBBS in Georgia
- MBBS in Vietnam
- MBBS in Kyrgyzstan
- MBBS in Kazakhstan
- MBBS in Uzbekistan
- MBBS in Bangladesh
- MBBS in Philippines

### 5. University pages

Upgrade the top-demand university pages with:

- fee clarity
- recognition context
- student fit
- who should avoid this university
- return-to-India implications
- local city context

## Tamil Nadu local cluster

### Goal

Capture search demand such as:

- MBBS abroad consultant in Chennai
- MBBS abroad counselling in Coimbatore
- study MBBS abroad from Madurai
- MBBS abroad guidance in Trichy

### Rule

Do not create thin doorway pages.

Each city page must contain real local value:

- city-specific user intent
- seminar proof where available
- parent concerns
- likely destination demand
- local FAQs
- internal links into country and compliance pages

### Priority local pages

- Chennai
- Coimbatore
- Madurai
- Trichy
- Salem
- Tirunelveli
- Erode
- Thoothukudi
- Vellore

## Internal linking model

- Home links to MBBS pillar pages, country pages, Tamil Nadu hub
- Tamil Nadu hub links to all city pages
- Each city page links to top country pages and seminar/contact routes
- Country pages link to fee and compliance pages
- Blog pages feed country and city pages
- University pages feed compare, budget, and return-to-India pages

## Execution order

### Phase 1

- Reposition homepage and messaging to MBBS-first
- Publish Tamil Nadu hub
- Publish first 6 to 9 Tamil Nadu city pages
- Upgrade top 4 MBBS pillar pages

### Phase 2

- Upgrade Russia, Georgia, Vietnam, Kyrgyzstan country pages
- Publish cost and compliance cluster
- Strengthen internal links between local and national pages

### Phase 3

- Upgrade top university pages
- Add original data assets
- Build backlinks using cost and compliance resources

## Assets to build later

- MBBS Abroad Cost Index
- India Return Roadmap
- University Fit Scorecard
- Parent Guide to MBBS Abroad

## Measurement

Track these separately:

- non-brand MBBS impressions
- Tamil Nadu location query impressions
- clicks into country pages from city pages
- counselling conversions from city pages
- seminar registrations from city pages
