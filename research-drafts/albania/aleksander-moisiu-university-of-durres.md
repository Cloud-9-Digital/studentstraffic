# Research record: Aleksandër Moisiu University of Durrës (UAMD) — Albania

Status: **NOT PUBLISHABLE — held (ok=false)**

## Why

UAMD does not offer any medical, dental, nursing, or pharmacy program. It has no
Faculty of Medicine, Faculty of Nursing/Health Sciences, or equivalent. The
publish gate requires `structuredFacts.programs` to contain at least one
program with an explicit `courseSlug` in `{mbbs, medical-pg, bsc-nursing,
pharmacy, bds}`. There is no honest way to populate that field for this
institution, so — per the exhaustive-then-omit rule — no program-bearing draft
can be produced. Fabricating a program would violate the hard "never
fabricate" rule, so this university is held as a non-match rather than
force-published.

## Key facts (for the record, corroborated across multiple independent sources)

- **Full name**: Aleksandër Moisiu University of Durrës (Universiteti
  "Aleksandër Moisiu" Durrës), commonly abbreviated **UAMD**.
- **City**: Durrës, Albania (Albania's second-largest city, main port city on
  the Adriatic coast, ~30 km / under an hour from Tirana).
- **Type**: Public university.
- **Established**: Founded by decision of the Albanian Council of Ministers on
  20 December 2005; began academic activity in 2006/2007 as the newest public
  university in Albania.
- **Official faculties (5)**, confirmed directly from the official site's
  primary navigation menu (https://uamd.edu.al/en/), each with its own page:
  1. Faculty of Business (Fakulteti i Biznesit) —
     https://uamd.edu.al/fakulteti-i-biznesit/
  2. Faculty of Education (Fakulteti i Edukimit) —
     https://uamd.edu.al/fakulteti-i-edukimit/
  3. Faculty of Professional Studies (Fakulteti i Studimeve Profesionale) —
     https://uamd.edu.al/fakulteti-i-studimeve-profesionale/ (checked directly
     for any hidden health/nursing/pharmacy track — none found; page lists no
     such program)
  4. Faculty of Political and Legal Sciences (Fakulteti i Shkencave Politike
     Juridike) — https://uamd.edu.al/fakulteti-i-shkencave-politike-juridike/
  5. Faculty of Information Technology (Fakulteti i Teknologjisë së
     Informacionit) —
     https://uamd.edu.al/fakulteti-i-teknologjise-se-informacionit/
- No Faculty of Medicine, Faculty of Dentistry, Faculty of Pharmacy, or
  Faculty of Nursing/Health Sciences exists at UAMD. This is corroborated
  independently by Wikipedia and by TopUniversities/UNIMED profile pages,
  which likewise list only business, education, professional studies,
  political-legal, and IT as UAMD's academic divisions.
- UAMD is noted (per its own site/history) for using elements of the American
  system of higher education, distinguishing it from Albania's older public
  universities.
