## Verdict / why held

This is **not** a civilian medical university with a commercial MBBS pathway for Indian students. It is a **federal state budgetary MILITARY educational institution of the Russian Ministry of Defence** ("Военно-медицинская академия имени С.М. Кирова" Министерства обороны Российской Федерации).

Its foreign-student unit is the **5th Faculty for training military-medical specialists of foreign armies** (Факультет подготовки военно-медицинских специалистов иностранных армий), created as a faculty in 1955 to train **foreign servicemen / military doctors** (historically e.g. Mongolian military physicians). Foreign citizens can enrol in specialitet / master / residency / postgraduate (DPO, adjunctura, doctorantura) programs, but this is a military-medical training track, not a civilian NEET-to-MBBS route.

Even aggregated third-party summaries acknowledge that "foreign intake is highly restricted and primarily reserved for students under intergovernmental agreements (Ministry of Defence quotas), not standard commercial admission."

Consultancy/agent sites market this as an "MBBS in Russia" destination, but their claims are **contradictory and uncorroborated**:
- Some claim English-medium MBBS + NMC/WHO approval; another claims applicants "must be Russian citizens."
- Several conflate this academy with the unrelated **civilian "Kirov State Medical University"** in Kirov Oblast (a different institution).

Because the following could **not** be corroborated from official / reliable sources, publishing an India-audience MBBS page would be fabrication and mis-framing:
- A civilian, commercial MBBS admission pathway open to Indian students.
- An official **English-medium** civilian MBBS program (official training is Russian-language, military-oriented).
- **Tuition / fees** for civilian foreign students (omitted entirely - not fabricated).
- **NMC recognition** for Indian licensing (not asserted; students told to verify with NMC).

## Corroborated facts (safe)

- **Type:** Public (state / Ministry of Defence). [Wikipedia + official]
- **Established:** 1798 (Medico-Surgical Academy, by order of Emperor Paul I). Origins trace to 1715/1798; renamed after Sergey Kirov in 1934. [Wikipedia]
- **Location:** Central Saint Petersburg, on the bank of the Neva River. [Wikipedia + official]
- **Mission:** Trains senior medical staff for the Russian Armed Forces; conducts military-medical research. [Wikipedia + official]
- **Structure (2002-era):** ~6 faculties, ~61-63 departments, ~30 clinics, research laboratories/centres. [Wikipedia]
- **Foreign faculty:** 5th Faculty for military-medical specialists of foreign armies (faculty since 1955). [official]
- **Official website:** www.vmeda.org (verified HTTP 200, 2026-07-07).
