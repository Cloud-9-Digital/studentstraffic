# Russian Medical Academy of Continuous Professional Education (RMANPO) — Research Notes

Status: **HELD — NOT PUBLISHED (ok=false)**
Country: Russia · City: Moscow
Research date: 2026-07-07

## Why this was held (not published)

RMANPO is, by its own founding charter and every corroborating source found, a
**postgraduate / continuing medical education academy** — it trains doctors who
already hold a medical degree (residency = "ординатура"/ordinatura, and PhD-level
research = "аспирантура"/aspirantura), plus continuing-education ("повышение
квалификации") short courses for practicing physicians. It has **no undergraduate
MBBS-equivalent ("специалитet" / "лечебное дело") program** — nothing a
pre-medical-degree Indian student could apply to directly out of high school.

This site's publishing gate requires each program to map to one of a fixed set of
`courseSlug` values: `mbbs, medical-pg, bsc-nursing, pharmacy, bds`. Forcing
RMANPO's Russian-language, Russia-licensed-doctors-oriented ordinatura into
`medical-pg` would misrepresent the program to the Indian-student audience this
pipeline targets (who read "medical PG" as a path comparable to NEET-PG/India-linked
postgraduate options). I could not corroborate:

- Any NMC/FMGE/WHO-linked recognition pathway for RMANPO's ordinatura relevant to
  Indian MBBS graduates re-entering Indian practice.
- Any English-medium instruction track for the ordinatura/aspirantura programs
  (all admissions/nav content found was Russian-only; no English program pages
  were live).
- Any undergraduate MBBS/specialitet program at all — the academy's own
  structure, history and admissions navigation (both the current site and the
  Russian Wikipedia entry) list only ординатура (residency), аспирантура
  (postgraduate/PhD), and профессиональная переподготовка/повышение
  квалификации (professional retraining/CPE) — never a first (undergraduate)
  medical degree.

Per the exhaustive-then-omit rule: rather than fabricate an MBBS program or
mis-slug a postgraduate-only, Russian-language residency program as
"medical-pg" for an Indian pre-degree audience, this draft is held. If a future
research pass finds a genuine, English-medium, internationally-recognized
program suited to the site's audience, this should be revisited.

## Corroborated facts (multi-source)

- **Full name (Russian):** Федеральное государственное бюджетное образовательное
  учреждение дополнительного профессионального образования «Российская
  медицинская академия непрерывного профессионального образования»
  Министерства здравоохранения Российской Федерации.
- **English name:** Russian Medical Academy of Continuous(/Continuing)
  Professional Education, Ministry of Healthcare of the Russian Federation
  (RMANPO).
- **Founded:** 1 December 1930, as the "State Moscow Institute for the
  Improvement and Specialization of Doctors and Healthcare Organizers."
  Renamed Central Institute for the Advanced Training of Physicians (ЦИУВ) in
  1931; awarded the Order of Lenin in 1966 (ЦОЛИУВ); reorganized as the Russian
  Medical Academy of Postgraduate Education (РМАПО) in 1994; became RMANPO in
  2016 when four regional postgraduate medical institutes (Irkutsk, Kazan,
  Novokuznetsk, Penza) merged in as branches.
- **Location:** Moscow, ul. Barrikadnaya 2/1-1, 125993, Russian Federation.
- **Type:** Public / state federal institution (Ministry of Health of Russia),
  not a fee-driven private university.
- **Nature of programs:** Postgraduate-only — clinical residency ("ординатура"),
  postgraduate doctoral studies ("аспирантура"), and continuing/professional
  retraining (повышение квалификации, профессиональная переподготовка) for
  already-qualified physicians and healthcare educators. Confirmed via the
  academy's own site navigation (rmanpo.ru — "Абитуриенту 2026 (ординатура,
  аспирантура)") and the Russian Wikipedia structure/history sections.
- **Rector:** Viktor Viktorovich Fomin (2025–present, per news search results),
  succeeding Dmitry Alekseevich Sychev (rector 30 Apr 2019 – 2025, per Russian
  Wikipedia and multiple Medvestnik news articles).
- **International/foreign students:** RMANPO does host foreign students, but
  per its own "Информация для иностранных обучающихся" (Information for Foreign
  Students) page, this is specifically for continuous/professional medical
  education (postgraduate), on a fee-paying (внебюджетная) basis under
  contracts — consistent with the postgraduate-only scope, not an undergraduate
  MBBS pathway.
- **Historic international role:** From the early 1960s the institute trained
  healthcare personnel/administrators from Asia, Africa and Latin America; WHO's
  Moscow International Courses for Hospital Administrators have run there since
  1963 (per search-engine synthesis of official-site history content).

## What was OMITTED and why

- **Any MBBS/undergraduate program listing** — omitted because none exists;
  fabricating one would violate the no-fabrication rule.
- **Tuition/fees** — not researched in depth once the program-fit gate failure
  was clear; would in any case only apply to postgraduate/CPE programs, not to
  an MBBS-shaped landing page.
- **Hostel/campus lifestyle specifics for an MBBS-style page** — the academy
  does have listed hostels (Moscow, ul. Smolnaya 40 and Leningradskoye Shosse
  106) per its own site navigation, but building a full "campus life for Indian
  MBBS students" narrative would misrepresent the actual (postgraduate,
  Russian-medium) student population.
- **English-medium claims** — omitted; the only site version found under the
  `/en/` path resolved to a Russian-language 404 page, so no evidence of a live
  English program track could be corroborated.

## Recommendation

Do not publish a page for RMANPO under the current MBBS/BDS/Pharmacy/Nursing
course-slug taxonomy. If the site later adds a course category for
Russia-based postgraduate medical residency/fellowship aimed at doctors (not
pre-degree students), RMANPO would be a strong, well-documented, easily
re-researchable candidate at that time.
