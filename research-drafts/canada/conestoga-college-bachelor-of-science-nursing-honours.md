# Research Record — Conestoga College, Bachelor of Science - Nursing (Honours)

**Status: HELD (ok=false) — not published, not seeded.**

## Reason for hold

This program is a **direct-entry, Ontario-residents-only degree**. Multiple independent
sources corroborate that international applicants (including students from India) are not
eligible for the standard Bachelor of Science - Nursing (Honours) direct-entry pathway:

- The official program page displays only a single "SEP 2026 | Kitchener – Doon" intake,
  which the page's own legend marks with the "Closed" status, and the "Apply" flow routes to
  **"Applying as a Canadian applicant" / "Domestic students should apply online or by phone at
  1-888-892-2228."** There is no parallel "International applicant" apply button/pathway shown
  on this program's apply widget (contrast with other Conestoga programs that show both
  Canadian and International offering codes side by side).
- Independent aggregator/search corroboration (multiple queries, cross-checked) consistently
  states: "Students living outside of Ontario are not eligible to apply" for this BScN, and
  that direct entry into the nursing degree is not available to international students — who
  are instead pointed to the **Pre-Health Sciences Pathway to Certificates and Diplomas** as an
  indirect route, not the BScN itself.
- Conestoga's own International Admissions guidance, when queried, could not produce an
  International-track apply link for this specific program (unlike, e.g., diploma programs
  that explicitly list international intakes/campuses).

Given the project's India-audience purpose (helping Indian students find and apply to
genuinely accessible international nursing programs), publishing a full recruitment-style page
for a program that Indian applicants cannot actually enroll in would be misleading — even with
caveats, the entire page's premise (help a student apply here) would be false. Per the hard
rule "never fabricate, honest caveats, EXHAUSTIVE-THEN-OMIT," the correct action is to **hold**
this university/program rather than publish it, since the discrepancy is not a matter of a
missing minor fact (like tuition) but a foundational eligibility mismatch.

## What WAS corroborated (for the record / future reference)

- **Institution**: Conestoga College (Institute of Technology and Advanced Learning), a public
  Ontario college, multiple campuses; the nursing degree is delivered at the **Kitchener – Doon**
  campus.
- **Program**: Bachelor of Science - Nursing (Honours), a 4-year (8-term) applied
  baccalaureate nursing degree. Conestoga was granted ministerial consent to offer this degree
  for a seven-year term starting December 1, 2023. The program has preliminary approval from
  the College of Nurses of Ontario (CNO); graduates are eligible to apply for RN registration in
  Ontario upon completion and licensing exam success.
- **Admission competitiveness**: highly competitive; reported minimum entering average around
  89.6% for a recent (Fall 2026) cycle; application deadline February 1 for September intake.
- **Eligibility restriction (the reason for the hold)**: "Students living outside of Ontario are
  not eligible to apply" — sourced from the official admissions messaging and corroborated by
  independent secondary sources. International applicants are directed toward indirect pathways
  (e.g., Pre-Health Sciences Pathway; Enhanced Practice for Internationally Educated Nurses
  graduate certificate — a different, post-licensure program for already-qualified nurses, not a
  substitute for this degree).
- **English proficiency thresholds cited generally by Conestoga International** (used across
  many Conestoga programs, not confirmed as applicable here given the above): TOEFL iBT 88,
  IELTS 6.5 (no band <6.0), CAEL 70, PTE Academic 58.
- Immunization/TB testing requirements are standard for clinical placements (MMR, Tdap,
  varicella, COVID-19, Hepatitis B, TB test) — standard for any Ontario nursing program, not
  specific corroboration of international eligibility.

## Recommendation

Hold this university/program out of the auto-publish queue. If the India-audience site wants to
cover Conestoga College nursing pathways for international students, the correct subject would
be a **different, genuinely international-eligible program** (e.g., the Pre-Health Sciences
Pathway to Certificates and Diplomas, or the Enhanced Practice for Internationally Educated
Nurses graduate certificate, if that one is confirmed open to internationally educated/qualified
nurses from India) — not this direct-entry BScN Honours degree.
