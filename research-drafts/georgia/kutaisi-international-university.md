## Key verified facts

- Public research university in Kutaisi, Georgia; opened October 2020, first cohort autumn 2020 (KIU official admission page + Wikipedia).
- Developed in strategic partnership with the **Technical University of Munich (TUM)**; honorary president **Wolfgang A. Herrmann** (former TUM president); rector **Vakhtang Tsagareli**; chancellor **Levan Kopaliani**; 1500+ students (Wikipedia + official).
- Also partners with University of Naples Federico II and University of Bamberg (official admission page).
- **School of Medicine** offers a **single-cycle Medical Doctor (MD)** program:
  - Language of instruction: **English** (official program page).
  - Duration: **12 semesters / 6 years, 360 ECTS** (official program page: "Program Duration: 12 semesters"; NCEQE report + secondary sources corroborate 360 ECTS / 10,800 hours).
  - **Double diploma** with **University of Naples Federico II** (official program page).
  - Georgian applicants admitted via Unified National Examinations (NAEC); international students via separate admission route.
- Program has been through **NCEQE** (Georgia's National Center for Educational Quality Enhancement) accreditation review (eqe.ge expert reports, incl. Dec 2024).

## IMPORTANT disambiguation

Many search results conflate three distinct institutions. Facts here are scoped **only** to KIU (kiu.edu.ge, public, founded 2020, TUM partnership):
- **Kutaisi International University (KIU)** - public, kiu.edu.ge - THIS university.
- **Kutaisi University (UNIK)** - separate private university.
- **Central University of Europe / "Kutaisi University"** (cue.edu.ge, est. 1991) - separate private university.
