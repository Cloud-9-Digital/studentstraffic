# Research Record: "Batumi Medical Academy (BMEDA)" — Batumi, Georgia

**Status: HELD / NOT PUBLISHABLE (ok = false)**
**Researched: 2026-07-07**

## Bottom line

Batumi Medical Academy (BMEDA) is a **vocational education (VET) college**, not a
higher-education medical university. It is authorized and regulated in Georgia
under the **"Board of Authorization of Vocational Education Institutions"**
(BMEDA's own website lists authorization decisions from 2014, 2015, 2018 (x2),
and 2019 under this exact regulatory heading). It is a member of the
**Association of Private Colleges of Georgia**.

Its only programs, per its own "Educational programs" page, are:
- **Pharmacy** (vocational/certificate level)
- **Dental Technician Program** (vocational/certificate level)
- **Nursing education** (vocational/certificate level)

These are sub-degree vocational/certificate programs delivered in Georgian for
the domestic Georgian healthcare workforce (technicians, vocational nurses,
pharmacy assistants) — **not** internationally recognized bachelor's or
professional degrees. There is no MBBS/MD program, no BDS program, no
bachelor's-level BSc Nursing degree, and no Pharmacy degree (BPharm/PharmD)
of the kind Indian students seek abroad.

Because this site's publish gate requires an explicit `courseSlug` in
`{mbbs, medical-pg, bsc-nursing, pharmacy, bds}` mapped to a genuine **degree**
program, and because none of BMEDA's offerings are degree programs, **no
program entry could be honestly constructed**. Asserting NMC/PCI/INC relevance
for a vocational-college certificate would be a fabrication and is explicitly
prohibited by the project's sourcing rules.

## Why the name is confusing (and worth flagging for future research)

Batumi has at least three distinctly named institutions that are easy to
conflate:

1. **Batumi Medical Academy (BMEDA)** — the vocational college researched
   here. `bmeda.edu.ge`
2. **Batumi Shota Rustaveli State University (BSU)** — a public state
   university in Batumi that does offer an MBBS/medical program and appears
   in Indian MBBS-abroad consultant listings with FMGE performance data.
   `bsu.edu.ge`
3. **Avicenna Batumi Medical University** — a newer (est. 2022) private
   medical university in Batumi that markets itself directly to Indian
   students, claiming WHO/NMC/FAIMER/AACI/WFME recognition and WDOMS listing.
   `avicennabatumi.edu.ge`

If Batumi coverage is wanted for the site, **Batumi Shota Rustaveli State
University** or **Avicenna Batumi Medical University** are the correct
candidates to research as separate entries — not BMEDA.
