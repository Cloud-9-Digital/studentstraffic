# Research Record: Telavi Iakob Gogebashvili State University — "Faculty of Healthcare (Public Health)"

Status: **NOT PUBLISHED (ok=false)** — held as a local draft only.

Country slug: `georgia` | City: Telavi | Research date: 2026-07-07

## Bottom line

The requested unit — a "Faculty of Healthcare" / "Public Health" faculty at Telavi Iakob
Gogebashvili State University (TESAU) — could **not be corroborated as a currently active
academic faculty**. Multi-source research (official site current navigation/catalogue,
Wikipedia's full faculty listing, third-party directories, and an EU vocational-program news
item) all agree that TESAU's current faculty structure (2026) consists of exactly three
faculties, none of which is healthcare-related:

1. Faculty of Education and Humanities (განათლებისა და ჰუმანიტარულ მეცნიერებათა ფაკულტეტი)
2. Faculty of Social Sciences, Business and Law (სოციალურ მეცნიერებათა, ბიზნესისა და სამართლის ფაკულტეტი)
3. Faculty of Agrarian, Natural Sciences and Technologies (აგრარულ, საბუნებისმეტყველო მეცნიერებათა და ტექნოლოგიების ფაკულტეტი)

This is confirmed independently by:
- The official site's live navigation menu / current program catalogue page
  (`https://tesau.edu.ge/saganmanatleblo-programebis-katalogi`), which lists only the
  three faculties above under "ფაკულტეტები" (Faculties) and contains **zero** matches
  for "ჯანდაცვ" (healthcare) or "ფარმაც" (pharmacy) anywhere on the page.
- The English Wikipedia article's exhaustive Bachelor's/Master's/PhD faculty lists (which
  cites TESAU's own "Bachelor Programmes" / "Master Programmes" / "Doctoral Programmes"
  pages as references) — again, no healthcare/medical/pharmacy/nursing faculty appears at
  any degree level.
- The official "State-funded Educational Programs" announcement, listing only History,
  Georgian Language and Literature, Biology, Food Technology, Mathematics, Primary
  Education, and Economics — no health-related program.
- A live web search for "ჯანდაცვის ფაკულტეტი" (Faculty of Healthcare) plus Telavi
  returned no current corroboration; the only hits pointing to a Telavi "health faculty"
  led back to a static/archived PDF directory (see below), not any live page.

## What we DID find (historical/archival evidence)

An old static file directory on the same official domain
(`https://tesau.edu.ge/failebi/jandacva/`) does contain files whose internal document
text refers to a "jandacvis fakulteti" ("Faculty of Healthcare") at TESAU:

- `bakalavriati/programebi/farmacia baka.pdf` — a **Bachelor of Pharmacy** curriculum
  document (240 ECTS), curriculum "created 2007, updated 2008," program head
  assoc. prof. Lali Mekokishvili, co-head Tamaz Murtazashvili (Doctor of Pharmaceutical
  Sciences). Mentions a student/staff exchange link with the University of Freiburg's
  (Germany) Faculty of Medicine.
- `bakalavriati/doqtorantura/doqt sazogadoebrivi jandacva.pdf` — a **PhD ("Doctorate")
  program titled "Public Health" / "PhD in Health Care"**, with committee-approval pages
  dated 2011, delivered partly via an inter-university memorandum with **Tbilisi State
  Medical University's International School of Public Health** (i.e., some modules are
  taught at TSMU, not at Telavi itself). Program leads: Prof. Zurab Rainauli, Prof. Lali
  Mekokishvili, invited Prof. Giorgi Kamkamidze.
- `profesiuli/` — professional-level (sub-degree) documents: "farm tana.pdf" (pharmacist's
  assistant) and two "kbilis teqn" (dental technician) levels 4 and 5 documents.

All files in this directory tree carry the **same server "last modified" timestamp of
2020-01-03**, which is consistent with a one-time bulk file-server migration rather than an
active update — it does not by itself prove the programs were active as recently as 2020,
let alone today. Crucially, none of this material is linked from, or mentioned on, any
current top-level navigation page, current program catalogue, current admissions page, or
current faculties page of the live site. There is also no current-day tuition, admission
cycle, enrollment status, current faculty roster, or NCEQE accreditation-status page found
for a "Faculty of Healthcare" at TESAU in 2026.

A separate, unrelated, and clearly-current item was found: an EU-supported vocational
"Laboratory Health and Safety" continuing-education course (2020s), but this sits under
TESAU's Faculty of Exact and Natural Sciences track, is a short professional-skills course
(not a degree program in medicine/nursing/pharmacy/dentistry), and does not correspond to
any of the target course types (mbbs, medical-pg, bsc-nursing, pharmacy, bds).

## Why this fails the honest-publish gate

- We cannot corroborate the Faculty of Healthcare, or any Public Health / Pharmacy /
  Medicine / Nursing / Dentistry bachelor's-or-above program, as **currently active** at
  Telavi State University. Publishing a page asserting Indian-student-relevant admissions
  facts (tuition, intake, current curriculum, current recognition) for a program we can
  only trace to 2007-2011 archived PDFs would risk misleading prospective students.
  Since the target audience is students planning study abroad in 2026-27, presenting a
  possibly-defunct program as available would be actively harmful, not just incomplete.
- The one degree that could technically map to a courseSlug (`pharmacy`, Bachelor of
  Pharmacy, 240 ECTS) has no current corroboration of being open for admission, no current
  tuition, no current faculty contact, and appears nowhere on the live site outside the
  static archive. Per the sourcing rules (multi-source, exhaustive-then-omit, never
  fabricate currency), this is insufficient to publish as a live, applicable-today program.
- The Public Health item found is a research PhD (not an entry professional degree for
  Indian students seeking MBBS/BDS/Nursing/Pharmacy pathways) delivered partly through
  Tbilisi State Medical University, so even if it were confirmed current, it would not fit
  the target course taxonomy or the target Indian-student MBBS/allied-health audience.
