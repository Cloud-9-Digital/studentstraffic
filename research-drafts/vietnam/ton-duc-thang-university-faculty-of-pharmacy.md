# Ton Duc Thang University - Faculty of Pharmacy (Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam)

**Research outcome: HOLD - not publish-ready.** Saved as a local research record only; not seeded/published.

## Key facts (corroborated)

- **University**: Ton Duc Thang University (TDTU), a large public university headquartered in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.
- **Faculty**: Faculty of Pharmacy, located at Room C008, 19 Nguyen Huu Tho Street, Tan Hung Ward, Ho Chi Minh City.
- **Contact**: pharmacy@tdtu.edu.vn, (028) 37761043.
- **Faculty scale**: 36 full-time lecturers (per faculty homepage counter; a separate WebSearch summary cited "30 full-time lecturers... including 1 Professor, 3 Associate Professors, 13 Doctors, and 13 Masters" - close but not identical figures, likely reflecting different snapshot dates), ~1,646 students, 140 ISI/Scopus articles, 60 partners (per faculty homepage stats).
- **Program**: Bachelor of Pharmacy, program code **7720201**, "standard program" (2019 curriculum), **5-year duration** (confirmed explicitly on TDTU's official admissions site: "The Major of Pharmacy requires 05 years of study duration").
- **Curriculum structure**: 37 general-curriculum credits (political theory, social science, English community courses, informatics, support skills, physical education, national defense education) + 151 specialized-education credits (Pharmaceutics & Pharmaceutical Industry, Drug Analysis & Quality Control, Pharmacology & Clinical Pharmacy, Pharmacognosy & Pharmaceutical Botany, Pharmaceutical Administration & Management, Microbiology-Parasitology-Biotechnology, Pharmaceutical Chemistry).

## The critical finding: Pharmacy is Vietnamese-medium only

TDTU publishes three official, mutually exclusive lists of undergraduate majors by language of instruction for the 2026-2027 academic year:

1. **"Undergraduate Programs Taught in English Language"** (11 majors) - Business Administration (4 variants), Finance-Banking, Accounting (International Accounting), Automation & Control Engineering, Information Technology, Software Engineering, Computer Science, Civil Engineering, English Language, Biotechnology. **Pharmacy is NOT on this list.**
2. **"Undergraduate Programs Taught in Vietnamese Language and English Language"** (19 majors, 50% Vietnamese / 50% English) - various Business, Finance, Engineering, Social Sciences, Law, Science majors. **Pharmacy is NOT on this list.**
3. **"Undergraduate Programs Taught in Vietnamese Language"** (52 majors) - includes, under **"HEALTH SCIENCE > Pharmacy > Pharmacy"**, with the explicit key-fact note: *"The Major of Pharmacy requires 05 years of study duration."* **This is the only list Pharmacy appears on.**

TDTU's official "Entry Requirements for undergraduate international students" page confirms that Vietnamese-medium programs require a **Vietnamese language proficiency certificate at B2 CEFR level** (with a compulsory ~1-year Vietnamese-language preparatory course for applicants who don't already hold B2). The same page adds Pharmacy-specific extra requirements for international applicants: last-year high-school GPA marked "Good" or above, and an average Chemistry score of >=7.0/10 in the final year of senior high school.

There is no confirmed English-medium admissions pathway into TDTU's Pharmacy program.

## Regulator recognition (India)

Checked the Pharmacy Council of India's (PCI) published lists of approved foreign/degree institutions - **no listing for Ton Duc Thang University was found.** No PCI recognition claim can be made.

## What was OMITTED and why

Per the sourcing rules (exhaustive-then-omit, never fabricate; correct regulator only if corroborated; honest caveats on language of instruction), the following were omitted rather than fabricated:

- **`structuredFacts.programs`**: left empty. A `pharmacy` courseSlug entry would require an English-medium `officialProgramUrl`, confirmed medium of instruction, and duration aimed at an India/international audience - none of which exists for this Vietnamese-medium program. Populating it anyway would misrepresent the university's own official admissions pages.
- **`bestFitFor`**, **`whyChoose`**, **`thingsToConsider`**, **`faq`**: left empty, since any India-audience "why choose" or FAQ content would need to reference a program pathway (English-medium Pharmacy admission) that does not exist.
- **All 8 narrative fields** (`summary`, `campusLifestyle`, `cityProfile`, `clinicalExposure`, `hostelOverview`, `indianFoodSupport`, `safetyOverview`, `studentSupport`): left empty. Writing India-audience narrative content (hostel life, Indian food support, clinical exposure framed for English-speaking Indian pharmacy students, PCI-relevant regulator guidance) would require fabricating a program pathway that isn't corroborated.
- **Faculty-founding year**: TDTU's own founding year (1997) is used in `structuredFacts.establishedYear`, but the Faculty of Pharmacy's own specific founding year within TDTU was not independently corroborated across sources, so no faculty-specific founding-year claim is made in narrative text.
- **PCI/NMC/INC regulator statement**: omitted entirely (no positive or negative claim asserted in narrative fields) since this is a hold record with no narrative content; the PCI check is documented here only as a research note.
- **Tuition fee**: not included. One secondary aggregator cited an estimated 80,000,000 VND/year "Phase 1" figure for international students, but this was not corroborated against an official TDTU fee schedule specific to Pharmacy, so it is omitted rather than asserted as fact.
