## Programs

### Pharmacy (Duoc hoc, code 7720201)
- Duration: **4 years** (explicitly marketed as "graduate in just 4 years", i.e., shorter than the more typical 5-year pharmacy track elsewhere)
- Curriculum: Vietnamese-medium. English appears only as "English 1" through "English 6" language-subject courses; core science/pharmacy courses (Anatomy, Pharmacology, Pharmacognosy, Medicinal Chemistry, Clinical Pharmacy, etc.) show no stated medium of instruction other than Vietnamese, and mandatory Vietnamese state-curriculum courses appear (Ho Chi Minh Thought, Marxist-Leninist Philosophy, Military Education, General Law).
- Admission (4 methods, all domestic): (1) Grade 10-12 transcript average of 3 subjects; (2) 2026 National High School Graduation Examination results; (3) 2026 VNU-HCM (or other) Competency Assessment Test; (4) Direct admission per Ministry of Education and Training (MOET) regulations.
- Admission threshold (per Q&A page): Grade-12 rating "excellent" or graduation-exam score >= 8.0.
- Tuition: preliminary Semester-1 fee cited in search-summary as 10,500,000 VND for Pharmacy/Nursing majors (secondary source, not independently confirmed on an official fee schedule page during this research pass -- **omitted from structured facts** rather than treated as authoritative).
- Official program URL: https://yersin.edu.vn/en/pharmacy/

### Nursing (Dieu duong, code 7720301)
- Duration: **3 years** (154-158 credits across Year 1/2/3: 54+57+47)
- Curriculum: Vietnamese-medium, with "English 1-5 / Japanese 1-5" offered as alternative language-subject courses (student choice of English or Japanese track for the language component only, not for core nursing instruction).
- Admission (same 4 domestic methods as Pharmacy).
- Admission threshold (per Q&A page): Grade-12 rating "good" or higher, or graduation-exam score >= 6.5.
- Notable feature: paid international internship opportunities for Yersin's own students, including up to 6 months in Japan (cited earnings up to VND 200 million) and clinical placement at "University of Medicine and Pharmacy Hospital, Ho Chi Minh City" as part of the "Business Semester" (semesters 3/6/9, 200+ partner companies/institutions).
- Official program URL: https://yersin.edu.vn/en/nursing/

## Why held (gate failure -- exhaustive-then-omit)

This draft cannot pass the publish gate honestly because, despite exhaustive research across the official site (English pages), independent Vietnamese news, and TTC Group's own coverage, **no evidence was found of**:

1. **English-medium instruction** for either Pharmacy or Nursing. Every curriculum table lists "English" (or "Japanese") only as a discrete language-subject course, not as the medium for core science/clinical courses. All other listed courses appear to be taught in Vietnamese (evidenced by mandatory Vietnamese state-curriculum subjects like Ho Chi Minh Thought, Marxist-Leninist Philosophy/Political Economy, Military Education).
2. **An international or Indian-student admissions pathway.** All four admission methods on both program pages, and on the general Academic Programs / Q&A pages, are domestic Vietnamese routes (high-school transcript average, National High School Graduation Exam, VNU-HCM Competency Assessment Test, MOET-regulated direct admission). No separate international-applicant admissions process, no foreign-qualification-equivalency route (unlike some other Vietnamese universities that explicitly accept SAT/A-Level/IB), and no India-specific content of any kind appears anywhere on the official site.
3. **PCI/INC-relevant recognition information for a foreign cohort.** The "International Cooperation" news page lists only outbound partnerships/exchanges (MOUs with Suranaree University of Technology in Thailand, Muhammadiyah University of Yogyakarta in Indonesia, and partnerships with Japanese/Korean institutions) benefiting Yersin's own (Vietnamese-admitted) students -- none describe an inbound admissions channel for foreign students to study Pharmacy or Nursing at Yersin.

Per the project's exhaustive-then-omit rule, writing any of the 8 required India-audience narrative fields (summary, campus lifestyle, city profile, clinical exposure, hostel overview, Indian food support, safety overview, student support) would require fabricating an admissions pathway, medium of instruction, or international-student support infrastructure that could not be corroborated. The draft is therefore held with all narrative fields and the `programs`/`bestFitFor` arrays left empty, consistent with the pattern already established for the other three held Vietnam drafts in this batch (Hai Duong Medical Technical University, HUTECH Faculty of Medicine, Nam Dinh University of Nursing).

**Revisit condition:** if Yersin University publishes explicit English-medium admissions terms and a genuine international/Indian-student admissions pathway for Pharmacy or Nursing (e.g., a dedicated international-admissions office page, foreign-qualification-equivalency route, or partnership specifically for Indian students), re-research and populate the draft.
