## Why this is on hold

1. **General Medicine (Y khoa)**: brand new, Ministry-approved 26 May 2026
   (Decision No. 1372/QD-BGDDT), first intake 2026 with only 50 students,
   Vietnamese-medium instruction only, domestic-admission-only pathways (no
   English-medium, no India-specific track). Full detail in the prior draft.
2. **Traditional Medicine (Y hoc co truyen)**: NOT yet an actively enrolling
   program. Fresh search confirms the programs actually admitting students in
   2026 under HUTECH's new "Truong Y va Khoa hoc Suc khoe" (School of
   Medicine and Health Sciences) are: Y khoa (General Medicine, newly
   opened), Duoc hoc (Pharmacy), Dieu duong (Nursing), and Ky thuat xet
   nghiem y hoc (Medical Laboratory Technology). Traditional Medicine and
   Dentistry (Rang Ham Mat) are explicitly described as programs HUTECH
   **plans to open** once quality-assurance conditions are completed —
   i.e., pending, not yet open, not yet admitting anyone.

Publishing an India-audience MBBS-abroad-style page for either program would
require fabricating facts (student life, clinical exposure, hostel
arrangements for foreign students, Indian food access, safety framing, NMC/
NEET/FMGE recognition angle) that cannot be corroborated from any official
or independent source. Per project sourcing rules (exhaustive-then-omit,
never fabricate), this is held as a local research record only.

## Key verified facts

- **University**: HUTECH (Truong Dai hoc Cong nghe TP.HCM), private
  university founded 26 April 1995 (Prime Ministerial Decision No. 235/TTg).
  Multiple Ho Chi Minh City campuses. ISO 9001:2015 and AUN-QA quality
  frameworks; QS 4-Star overall rating (Nov 2022); QS World University
  Rankings 2027 band 1401+.
- **School of Medicine and Health Sciences**: established as an internal
  grouping on 2 June 2026, covering Medicine, Pharmacy, Nursing, and Medical
  Laboratory Technology.
- **General Medicine program**: 6 years, 210 credits, "Bac si Y khoa"
  degree, code 7720101, approved 26 May 2026, first cohort 50 students,
  admission via Vietnam's domestic national-exam / transcript / V-SAT /
  VNU-HCM aptitude-test methods (SAT/ACT/A-Level/IB accepted only as a
  general, non-medicine-specific equivalency route).
- **Traditional Medicine program**: named alongside General Medicine and
  Dentistry as part of HUTECH's health-sciences expansion plans, but per
  the most specific 2026 source found (Hoc Mai career-guidance summary),
  it is **not** among the programs actually recruiting/training in 2026 —
  HUTECH is still preparing the quality-assurance conditions required to
  open it. No confirmed intake year, no confirmed curriculum length, no
  confirmed medium of instruction, no confirmed admissions pathway.
- **Language of instruction**: every official HUTECH page found (including
  the `khoa-hoc-suc-khoe` faculty landing page, checked again in this pass)
  remains Vietnamese-language only, with repeated mentions of "Y hoc co
  truyen" but no English-medium or international-admissions content for
  either Medicine or Traditional Medicine.
