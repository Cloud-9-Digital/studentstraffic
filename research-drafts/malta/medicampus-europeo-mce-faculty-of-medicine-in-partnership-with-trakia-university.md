# MediCampus Europeo (MCE) - Faculty of Medicine, in partnership with Trakia University

Country: Malta | City: Floriana | Research date: 2026-07-07 | Status: Draft, gate-passing, ready for `seed-nonwdoms-draft.ts` + publish review

## Key facts (corroborated)

- **Institution**: MediCampus Europeo (MCE), a private medical faculty in Floriana, Malta. Campus at Casa Leone, Triq L-Imhazen, Il-Furjana FRN 1200, Malta — ~800 metres from Valletta.
- **Licensing**: MCE was licensed as a Higher Education Institution by Malta's Further and Higher Education Authority (MFHEA) on **26 June 2024** (Licence No. 2024-020). This is a very new institution — established/licensed year recorded as **2024**.
- **Academic partner**: Programme delivered in franchise/accreditation partnership with **Trakia University**, a Bulgarian state university founded in 1995, based in Stara Zagora (~9,000 students, 9 faculties, NEAA-accredited). The MD degree is issued by Trakia University, not by MCE directly.
- **Programme**: Doctor of General Medicine (MD), 6 years, 360 ECTS, 100% English-taught. Clinical training/clerkships from Year 3, across partner teaching hospitals in the EU (specific hospital list not itemised in the pages retrieved — MCE's dedicated "Teaching Hospitals" page 404'd at the URL attempted).
- **Tuition**: EUR 14,500/year (single instalment) or EUR 15,500/year (two instalments), per the official Faculty of Medicine page.
- **Intakes cited**: 25/26 July 2026 and 22/23 August 2026 (both online; second subject to availability).
- **Admissions**: EU applicants — standard home-country secondary qualification. Non-EU applicants (incl. Indian students) — no published India-specific page; must contact Admissions Office directly to verify recognition/eligibility of their qualification.
- **Recognition claims (official)**: EU Directive 2005/36/EC automatic recognition across EU/Norway/Switzerland/UK; Trakia University listed in WDOMS (FAIMER School ID F0001145), supporting ECFMG Certification/USMLE eligibility.
- **Dean**: Prof. Peter Labas, MD, PhD — formerly Dean of the Faculty of Medicine, Comenius University Bratislava (8 years).
- **Campus**: Casa Leone, historic Maltese building renovated 2017, ~1,300 sqm, five floors. Anatomy/chemistry/biology labs, lecture hall, library, study rooms, rooftop cafeteria with sea views. 50 study slots cited (small cohort).
- **Housing**: No university-run dormitory. Student Office helps students find apartments in Floriana, Valletta, and nearby areas (~10 min by bus); live alone or share.
- **City**: Floriana (historically Borgo Vilhena) — small fortified town directly adjoining Valletta; Floriana Lines fortifications begun 1636; Borgo Vilhena suburb founded 1724 by Grand Master de Vilhena; small population (~2,205 as of 2014 per Wikipedia).
- **Indian food**: No large Indian diaspora/student community in Malta generally, but multiple named Indian restaurants (Naan Bar, TukTuk, etc.) confirmed operating in Valletta, an ~800m walk from campus.
- **Safety**: Malta broadly framed as safe, low-to-moderate crime EU country per Numbeo-style indices (general country context, not MCE-specific).
- **NMC/NEET/FMGE**: Standard Indian regulatory framework applies per NMC's own site (NEET required before enrolling abroad; FMGE/NExT required to practise in India). NMC does not separately name MCE or Trakia University. Page instructs students to verify current status directly with NMC rather than asserting approval.

## What was OMITTED and why

- **Specific named partner teaching hospitals** — the official site references clerkships "across partner teaching hospitals within the European Union" from Year 3, and a secondary source mentioned Bratislava as one clinical-training location, but no definitive, current, itemised list of all partner hospitals was retrievable (the site's own "Teaching Hospitals" nav link 404'd). Omitted rather than fabricated; page instructs students to request the current list from Admissions.
- **India-specific admission pathway / quota / direct Class-XII entry claim** — not published anywhere on the official site. Not asserted; page explicitly states applicants must verify with the Admissions Office case-by-case.
- **Blanket NMC recognition / automatic Indian degree recognition** — not asserted. NMC's own official site does not name MCE or Trakia University; the page frames this as something students must independently verify with NMC before enrolling.
- **Specific current-year tuition guarantee** — fee is stated as sourced from the official page at time of research (EUR 14,500–15,500), but page advises reconfirming directly with MCE since these can change year to year.
- **Recognition badges / recognition links / similar-university slugs / teaching hospitals structured arrays** — left empty (no fabricated entries) since no verifiable structured data was available for these fields.
