## Key facts

- **Operator**: Digital Education Holdings Ltd (DEH), registered in Malta, Reg. No. C 82123. "EDU" / "EDU Medical" is DEH's consumer/education brand.
- **Type**: Private Institution of Higher Education, licensed under the Maltese Education Act, licence **2018-10**, originally granted by NCFHE (June 2018), now regulated by the **Malta Further and Higher Education Authority (MFHEA)**.
- **Established**: 2018 (licence date). EDU expected its **first Programme in Medicine graduates only in 2025** per the most recent official page reviewed -- a very young outbound track record.
- **Programme**: "Programme in Medicine" = 3-year **Bachelor of Medicine (B.Med., 180 ECTS, MQF/EQF Level 6)** + 2.5-year **Master of Medicine (M.Med., 162 ECTS, MQF/EQF Level 7)** = combined **5.5 years, ~342 ECTS**.
- **Delivery model (materially different from a normal MBBS)**: theoretical study is delivered online via EDU's own "digital campus" (small tutor/mentor-led groups, up to 5 students); clinical training happens in **4-week rotation blocks at partner teaching hospitals in Germany** (named partners: **Helios Kliniken**, **Marienhaus-Gruppe**). Clinical exposure starts ~Week 10 of the Bachelor; Master ends with a 6-month Clinical Clerkship.
- **Language**: English (B2) for theory; **German (C1 certificate required)** for clinical rotations, since teaching hospitals are in Germany -- waived only if the applicant's own prior schooling was in German. This is a significant, easily overlooked burden for Indian applicants.
- **Quality assurance**: MFHEA licensing; AHPGS (German Akkreditierungsrat-approved agency) accreditation of B.Med./M.Med. on 4 May 2023; supervision since Feb 2020 under **Article 24 of EU Directive 2005/36/EC** by **Maastricht University's Faculty of Health, Medicine and Life Sciences (FHML)**.
- **EU recognition status**: at the time of the most recent official page reviewed, EDU was still **"in discussions" with the Medical Council of Malta** to secure automatic EU recognition -- **not yet confirmed finalised**.
- **WDOMS**: No listing for EDU / Digital Education Holdings found. Checked several Malta-linked FAIMER/WDOMS IDs surfaced by search (F0005150 = Queen Mary Univ. of London/Barts Malta; F0009016 = ASOMI College of Sciences; others unrelated) -- none is EDU.
- **Location**: Euroguidance Malta's live government-linked directory currently lists EDU/DEH's address as **Chaplain's House, Villa Bighi, Marina Street, Kalkara KKR 1320**, locality filter "Kalkara". An older (2024-dated) Wayback capture of EDU's own site footer instead showed **Gzira** (SOHO The Strand, Fawwara Building, Triq L-Imsida). Used **Kalkara** as the more current, independently-corroborated locality; flagged the discrepancy plainly in researchNotes. Task brief named "St Julian's" -- not corroborated by any source found; not used.
- **Tuition**: EDU's own live site could not be reached (network timeout on medical.edu.mt, port 443) and no archived tuition-specific page was found. A third-party aggregator (future-doctor.eu) estimates **~EUR 19,500/year**, presented in the draft only as an attributed third-party estimate, not an official confirmed fee. No numeric tuition was asserted as fact in structuredFacts/programs.
- **Housing**: No EDU-run or EDU-recommended hostel/accommodation arrangement found (consistent with the online-study model). Stated as an honest information gap, not fabricated.
- **Admissions**: 5-step process -- online application, ITB Consulting online cognitive test, video interview, admission decision + contract/financing call, matriculation. Prerequisites: matriculation-level secondary certificate (Abitur/IB/A-Level or equivalent), police check, CV, cover letter, passport/ID, English B2, German C1 (for clinical training).
